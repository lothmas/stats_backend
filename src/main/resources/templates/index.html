How It Works?

E-Verify Step by Step process flow.

Register / Login

Step 1

Create an E-Verify Account and Activate via an email-link sent to your registered e-mail & login to the System.

Top Up Account

Step 2

Make a Payment Using your Username as Reference to Embase your Account. Payment Methods Include (1.) Cash Deposit (2.) EFT (3.) Debit / Credit

Authentication Request

Step 3

Make an Authentication Request and Receive your Results in real-time. If results can't be made in real-time then a notification that of the results will be sent out either via e-mail or sms.
